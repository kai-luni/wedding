Dress Code

Our wedding theme features three beautiful colors: cream, rose, and brown. We'd love for you to incorporate these theme colors into your outfit - whether it's a rose-colored dress, a cream suit, or a brown tie.

For the morning Thai ceremony, you're welcome to wear either Western formal attire or traditional Thai dress. If you'd like to wear traditional Thai clothing, please let us know in advance so we can help arrange it for you!

For the afternoon reception, please wear formal attire incorporating our theme colors. For the evening villa party, semi-formal attire is appropriate. Most importantly, wear something you feel comfortable and beautiful in - we want you to enjoy the celebration!
