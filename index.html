Our wedding theme features three beautiful colors: cream, rose, and brown. We'd love for you to incorporate these theme colors into your outfit - whether it's a rose-colored dress, a cream suit, or a brown tie.

For the afternoon reception and evening villa party, semi-formal to formal attire incorporating our theme colors would be perfect. Most importantly, wear something you feel comfortable and beautiful in - we want you to enjoy the celebration!
