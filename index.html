Dress Code

Our wedding theme features three beautiful colors: cream, rose, and brown. We'd love for you to incorporate these theme colors into your outfit - whether it's a rose-colored dress, a cream suit, or a brown tie.

Traditional Thai Dress Information

You have two options for the morning Thai ceremony:

  • Traditional Thai Dress: You can rent traditional Thai attire for approximately 10 Euro for the entire day. This is a wonderful way to experience the ceremony authentically.
  • Western Formal Attire: Alternatively, you can wear a suit or dress in our wedding colors (cream, rose, and brown).

For women choosing traditional Thai dress, professional makeup services are available at the venue for around 40 Euro. A separate dressing room will be provided at the venue for changing and makeup application.

For the afternoon reception, please wear formal attire incorporating our theme colors. For the evening villa party, semi-formal attire is appropriate. Most importantly, wear something you feel comfortable and beautiful in - we want you to enjoy the celebration!

Gift Giving

In accordance with Thai wedding tradition, monetary gifts are customary. Envelopes for cash gifts will be provided at the venue. Your presence is already the greatest gift, but if you wish to give something, this is the traditional approach.
